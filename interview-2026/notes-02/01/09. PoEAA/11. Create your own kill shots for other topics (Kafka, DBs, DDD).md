# 🔥 KAFKA / EVENT STREAMING — Kill Shots

### 1️⃣ *“Kafka doesn’t give me reliability; it gives me **time** to recover reliability downstream.”*

**Signals**

* You understand **durability vs correctness**
* You know Kafka doesn’t fix consumers
* You think in **failure windows**

---

### 2️⃣ *“If I need exactly-once semantics, I redesign the workflow before I trust the broker.”*

**Signals**

* You know EOS is scoped and fragile
* You model idempotency at the edges
* You don’t worship features

---

### 3️⃣ *“Consumer lag is not a bug; it’s backpressure made visible.”*

**Signals**

* Operational maturity
* Metrics literacy
* Backpressure awareness

---

### 4️⃣ *“Kafka throughput problems are usually serialization or partitioning mistakes, not Kafka problems.”*

**Signals**

* Hands-on tuning experience
* Awareness of hot partitions
* Practical debugging mindset
