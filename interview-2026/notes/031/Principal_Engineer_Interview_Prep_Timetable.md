## üí° Tips for Success

### Time Management
1. **Stick to schedule**: Consistency is key
2. **Quality over quantity**: Better 2 focused hours than 4 distracted hours
3. **Take breaks**: 5-10 min breaks every hour
4. **Adjust as needed**: Adapt schedule to your life

### Study Techniques
1. **Active recall**: Test yourself, don't just read
2. **Spaced repetition**: Review concepts multiple times
3. **Teach others**: Explain concepts to solidify understanding
4. **Practice problems**: Apply knowledge, don't just memorize

### Mental Health
1. **Get enough sleep**: 7-8 hours minimum
2. **Exercise regularly**: Physical health = mental clarity
3. **Take weekends off**: Rest is important
4. **Stay positive**: Believe in your preparation

### Interview Preparation
1. **Practice explaining**: Out loud, to others
2. **Time yourself**: Simulate real interview conditions
3. **Get feedback**: From peers, mentors, mock interviews
4. **Stay calm**: Confidence comes from preparation
