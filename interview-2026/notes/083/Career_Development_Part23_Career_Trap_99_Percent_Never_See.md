## Recognizing the Trap

### Warning Signs

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Trap Warning Signs                            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

You're in the trap if:
├─ Same role for 2+ years
├─ No significant growth
├─ Doing same type of work
├─ No new challenges
├─ Waiting for promotion
└─ Frustrated but no action
```

### Self-Assessment Questions

- Am I only doing assigned work?
- Am I meeting but not exceeding expectations?
- Am I waiting for recognition?
- Am I in my comfort zone?
- Am I creating visible impact?

If yes to 3+: You're likely in the trap
