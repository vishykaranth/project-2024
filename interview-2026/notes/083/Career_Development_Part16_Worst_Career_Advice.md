# The WORST Career Advice Devs Still Believe (And What To Do Instead)

## Overview

This video debunks common but harmful career advice that developers still follow, explaining why it's wrong and providing better alternatives for career success.

## The Worst Career Advice

### Advice 1: "Just Keep Your Head Down and Code"

#### Why It's Wrong

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Head Down Coding Problem                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

What Happens:
├─ Good work goes unnoticed
├─ No visibility
├─ Limited opportunities
└─ Career stagnation

Reality:
├─ Visibility matters for advancement
├─ Communication is essential
├─ Relationships drive opportunities
└─ Head down = invisible
```

#### What To Do Instead

- **Build visibility**: Share your work and achievements
- **Communicate effectively**: Explain what you're doing and why
- **Build relationships**: Network and collaborate
- **Document impact**: Measure and share results

### Advice 2: "Learn Every New Technology"

#### Why It's Wrong

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Learning Everything Problem                    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

What Happens:
├─ Surface-level knowledge
├─ No deep expertise
├─ Constant learning, no mastery
└─ Jack of all trades, master of none

Reality:
├─ Deep expertise is more valuable
├─ Fundamentals transfer across technologies
├─ Focus beats breadth
└─ Quality over quantity
```

#### What To Do Instead

- **Master fundamentals**: CS principles, system design
- **Deep expertise**: Become expert in specific areas
- **Strategic learning**: Learn technologies that add value
- **Apply principles**: Transfer knowledge across technologies

### Advice 3: "Job Hopping Will Hurt Your Career"

#### Why It's Wrong

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Staying Too Long Problem                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

What Happens:
├─ Stagnant skills
├─ Limited opportunities
├─ Lower salary growth
└─ Missed experiences

Reality:
├─ Strategic job changes accelerate growth
├─ New experiences build skills
├─ Market rates change faster than raises
└─ Loyalty doesn't guarantee advancement
```

#### What To Do Instead

- **Strategic moves**: Change jobs for growth, not just money
- **Stay 2-3 years minimum**: Show commitment but don't stagnate
- **Evaluate opportunities**: Growth, learning, impact
- **Build relationships**: Maintain network across companies

### Advice 4: "Technical Skills Are All That Matter"

#### Why It's Wrong

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Technical-Only Focus Problem                    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

What Happens:
├─ Great coder, poor communicator
├─ Can't influence decisions
├─ Limited to individual contributor
└─ Career plateau

Reality:
├─ Communication is critical
├─ Business understanding matters
├─ Leadership skills enable growth
└─ Soft skills differentiate
```

#### What To Do Instead

- **Develop communication**: Technical writing, presentations
- **Build business acumen**: Understand company and market
- **Develop leadership**: Mentoring, influence, decision-making
- **Balance skills**: Technical + business + soft skills

### Advice 5: "Work More Hours to Get Ahead"

#### Why It's Wrong

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Overwork Problem                               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

What Happens:
├─ Burnout
├─ Poor work-life balance
├─ Diminishing returns
└─ Health and relationship issues

Reality:
├─ Quality over quantity
├─ Sustainable pace wins long-term
├─ Efficiency beats hours
└─ Balance enables better performance
```

#### What To Do Instead

- **Work efficiently**: Focus on high-value work
- **Set boundaries**: Protect personal time
- **Maintain balance**: Health and relationships matter
- **Focus on impact**: Results over hours
