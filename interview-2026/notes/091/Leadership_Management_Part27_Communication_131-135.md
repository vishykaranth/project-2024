# Leadership & Management Answers - Part 27: Technical Communication (Questions 131-135)

## Question 131: How do you explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders?

### Answer

### Technical Communication Strategy

#### 1. **Communication Techniques**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Communication Techniques                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Use Analogies:
├─ Relate to familiar concepts
├─ Simple metaphors
└─ Real-world examples

Visual Aids:
├─ Diagrams
├─ Charts
├─ Flowcharts
└─ Infographics

Avoid Jargon:
├─ Simple language
├─ Explain terms
└─ Focus on impact

Structure:
├─ Executive summary
├─ Business impact
├─ Technical details (optional)
└─ Next steps
```
