```

- Single thread

- Multiple threads
- Throughput focused
- Higher pause times

G1 GC:
- Low pause times
- Large heaps
- Predictable pauses

- Ultra-low pauses (<10ms)
- Very large heaps (TB)
- Concurrent everything

- Ultra-low pauses (<10ms)
```
