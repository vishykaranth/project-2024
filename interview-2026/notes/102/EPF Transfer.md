- To initiate an EPF transfer request, log in to the EPFO Unified Portal with your UAN, go to 'Online Services' > 'One Member – One EPF Account (Transfer Request)', verify details, get previous employer's details, choose an employer (current/previous) to attest, get and submit an OTP, and then print, sign Form 13, and submit it to your chosen employer within 10 days for attestation and forwarding to EPFO for processing. 
- Step-by-step EPF Transfer Process:
- Login: Go to the EPFO member portal and log in using your UAN and password.
- Navigate: Click on 'Online Services' and select 'One Member – One EPF Account (Transfer Request)'.
- Verify Details: Check that your personal and current employment PF details are correct.
- Get Previous Details: Click 'Get Details' to view your previous employer's PF account information.
- Select Attester: Choose either your Current Employer or Previous Employer to attest the form, depending on who has a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) available.
- Get OTP: Click 'Get OTP' and enter the OTP sent to your UAN-registered mobile number.
- Submit: Click 'Submit' to get a tracking ID. Print and sign the generated Form 13.
- Employer Action: Submit the signed Form 13 to your chosen employer within 10 days for them to review and forward to EPFO for final approval. 
- Important Prerequisites:
- UAN Activated: Your UAN must be activated.
- e-KYC: Your Aadhaar, PAN, and bank account details must be seeded and approved (e-KYC) against your UAN.
- Date of Exit: The date of exit for your previous employment must be entered.
