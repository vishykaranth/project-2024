# Part 48: Architectural Characteristics - Quick Revision

## Operational Characteristics

- **Availability**: System uptime, redundancy, failover
- **Performance**: Response time, throughput, resource efficiency
- **Scalability**: Handle growth, horizontal/vertical scaling
- **Reliability**: Correctness, fault tolerance
- **Recoverability**: Recovery from failures, backup/restore

## Structural Characteristics

- **Modularity**: Separation into modules, high cohesion, low coupling
- **Configurability**: Externalize configuration, environment-specific
- **Extensibility**: Easy to add features, plugin architecture
- **Testability**: Easy to test, mockable dependencies
- **Maintainability**: Easy to understand and modify

## Cross-Cutting Characteristics

- **Security**: Authentication, authorization, encryption
- **Usability**: User experience, ease of use
- **Observability**: Logging, metrics, tracing
- **Deployability**: Ease of deployment, automation

## Frequently Overlooked

- **Observability**: Understanding system behavior, debugging
- **Elasticity**: Automatic scaling, dynamic capacity
- **Evolvability**: Ease of future modifications
- **Testability**: Testing effectiveness
- **Deployability**: Deployment ease and speed
