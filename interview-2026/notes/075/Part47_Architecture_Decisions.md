# Part 47: Architecture Decisions - Quick Revision

## Decision Framework

- **Identify Decision**: What decision needs to be made?
- **Gather Information**: Requirements, constraints, options, trade-offs
- **Evaluate Options**: Pros/cons, risks, costs
- **Make Decision**: Choose option, document rationale
- **Review Decision**: Monitor outcomes, reassess if needed

## ADR (Architecture Decision Record)

- **Title**: Decision name
- **Status**: Proposed, Accepted, Rejected, Deprecated
- **Context**: Why decision is needed
- **Decision**: What was decided
- **Consequences**: Positive and negative impacts
- **Alternatives**: Other options considered

## Overcoming Analysis Paralysis

- **Time-Boxing**: Set deadlines for decisions
- **Decision Criteria**: Define objective criteria, weight them
- **Good Enough**: Accept sufficient solutions, not perfect
- **Reversibility**: Distinguish reversible from irreversible decisions
- **Last Responsible Moment**: Make decisions when you have enough information
