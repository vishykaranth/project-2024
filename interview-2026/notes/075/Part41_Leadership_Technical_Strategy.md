# Part 41: Leadership & Technical Strategy - Quick Revision

## Principal Engineer Role

- **Technical Leadership**: Guide technical direction, architecture decisions
- **Mentoring**: Develop junior engineers, share knowledge
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Work with product, design, operations
- **Strategic Thinking**: Long-term vision, technology roadmap

## Architecture Decision Making

- **ADRs (Architecture Decision Records)**: Document decisions, rationale, consequences
- **Trade-off Analysis**: Evaluate options, consider constraints
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Get buy-in from teams, management
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify and mitigate technical risks

## Technical Strategy

- **Technology Evaluation**: Assess new technologies, proof of concepts
- **Technical Debt Management**: Balance new features vs debt reduction
- **Innovation**: Explore new approaches, stay current with trends
- **Standards & Best Practices**: Establish coding standards, patterns

## Communication

- **Technical Writing**: Clear documentation, architecture diagrams
- **Presentations**: Explain complex concepts to diverse audiences
- **Code Reviews**: Provide constructive feedback, maintain quality
- **Stakeholder Management**: Communicate with non-technical stakeholders
