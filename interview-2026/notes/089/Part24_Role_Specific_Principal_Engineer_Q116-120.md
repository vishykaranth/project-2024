## Question 120: What's the difference between a Senior Engineer and a Principal Engineer?

### Answer

Key differences:

**Senior Engineer:**
- Focus: Feature development, system design
- Scope: Individual services, features
- Influence: Team-level
- Mentoring: Junior engineers
- Time: 80% coding, 20% design

**Principal Engineer:**
- Focus: Architecture, technical strategy
- Scope: Multiple services, systems
- Influence: Organization-level
- Mentoring: All levels
- Time: 30% coding, 70% architecture

**Key Distinctions:**
- Scope: Local vs organizational
- Influence: Team vs organization
- Focus: Implementation vs strategy
- Impact: Feature vs system
